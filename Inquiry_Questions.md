# Client Discovery Questionnaires

This document contains the structured, step-by-step inquiry forms for all core digital services. Use these to build the multi-step "Start a Project" flow.

---

## 1. Web Application Development
*For complex platforms, custom software, and portals.*

### 1. The Big Picture
* **Question:** In simple terms, what do you want this app to do for your business?
* **Description:** Briefly describe the main problem this app solves or the primary goal you want it to achieve.
* **Input Type:** Textarea
* **Input Verification:** Required (Minimum 20 characters).

### 2. The Inspiration
* **Question:** Are there any existing apps or websites that do something similar to what you want?
* **Description:** E.g., "Like Uber, but for dog walking" or "A portal similar to Airbnb." Feel free to paste links!
* **Input Type:** Textarea
* **Input Verification:** Optional.

### 3. The Users
* **Question:** Who will be using this application?
* **Description:** Select all the types of people who will need to log in and interact with the system.
* **Input Type:** Checkbox
* **Input Verification:** Required (Must select at least 1).
* **Checkbox Options:**
    * Everyday Customers / Public Users
    * Internal Staff / Employees
    * Business Partners / Vendors
    * Other (We can discuss this on our call)

### 4. The "Must-Haves"
* **Question:** What are the top 3 essential features this app absolutely needs on day one?
* **Description:** If you had to launch with only three features to start testing your idea, what would they be?
* **Input Type:** Textarea
* **Input Verification:** Required.

### 5. The Content & Actions
* **Question:** What kind of actions will users primarily take inside the app?
* **Description:** Select the core functionalities you know you will need.
* **Input Type:** Checkbox
* **Input Verification:** Optional.
* **Checkbox Options:**
    * Booking / Scheduling Appointments
    * Processing Payments / Subscriptions
    * Uploading / Viewing Documents & Photos
    * Viewing Data Dashboards / Reports
    * Messaging / Internal Communication
    * I'm not entirely sure yet

### 6. The Connections
* **Question:** Does this app need to connect with any other tools you already use?
* **Description:** Let us know if we need to link your app to external services.
* **Input Type:** Checkbox
* **Input Verification:** Optional.
* **Checkbox Options:**
    * Payment Gateways (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
    * Email Marketing (Mailchimp, ActiveCampaign, etc.)
    * Accounting / CRM (QuickBooks, Salesforce, etc.)
    * Social Media Logins (Sign in with Google, Apple, etc.)
    * No, it will be a completely standalone app

### 7. The Timeline
* **Question:** What is your ideal launch timeline?
* **Description:** When do you need this application to be live and usable?
* **Input Type:** Radio Button
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Radio Options:**
    * As soon as possible (Urgent)
    * 1 - 3 months
    * 3 - 6 months
    * No rush, just exploring my options right now

### 8. The Budget
* **Question:** What is your allocated budget range for the initial launch (MVP)?
* **Description:** Building custom software is an investment. Knowing your budget helps us recommend the right technical approach.
* **Input Type:** Select (Dropdown)
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Selection Options:** * Under $3,000 (We might need to scale back features)
    * $3,000 - $5,000
    * $5,000 - $10,000
    * $10,000+

## 3. Mobile Application Development
*For iOS and Android native or cross-platform builds.*

### 1. The Core Purpose
* **Question:** What is the main problem this mobile app will solve for your users?
* **Description:** Give us a brief overview of what the app does and why someone would want to download it to their phone.
* **Input Type:** Textarea
* **Input Verification:** Required (Minimum 20 characters).

### 2. The Platform Target
* **Question:** Which devices do you want this app to be available on?
* **Description:** Building for Apple (iOS) and Android can require different approaches.
* **Input Type:** Radio Button
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Radio Options:**
    * Both iOS (Apple) and Android (Google Play)
    * Only iOS (Apple App Store)
    * Only Android (Google Play Store)
    * I'm not sure yet, let's discuss

### 3. The Hardware Features
* **Question:** Will the app need to access any native smartphone features?
* **Description:** Select any device capabilities your app will rely on to function properly.
* **Input Type:** Checkbox
* **Input Verification:** Optional.
* **Checkbox Options:**
    * Camera / Photo Library
    * GPS / Real-time Location Tracking
    * Push Notifications
    * Bluetooth / Connecting to external devices
    * Offline Mode (Needs to work without internet)

### 4. The Accounts & Security
* **Question:** How will users log in and manage their profiles?
* **Description:** Select the authentication methods you want to offer your users.
* **Input Type:** Checkbox
* **Input Verification:** Optional.
* **Checkbox Options:**
    * Standard Email & Password
    * Social Login (Continue with Google, Apple, Facebook)
    * Phone Number / OTP (One-Time Password)
    * No login required (Open access to everyone)

### 5. The Existing Infrastructure
* **Question:** Do you already have an existing website or database this app needs to connect to?
* **Description:** If you already have a web platform, we will need to connect the mobile app to your existing database.
* **Input Type:** Radio Button
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Radio Options:**
    * No, we are building everything completely from scratch
    * Yes, I have an existing website/database we need to connect to
    * I have some systems, but I'm not sure if they can connect

### 6. The Monetization Strategy
* **Question:** How do you plan to make money with this app?
* **Description:** This dictates whether we need to integrate complex App Store billing systems or standard payment gateways.
* **Input Type:** Radio Button
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Radio Options:**
    * It will be a 100% free app
    * In-App Purchases (e.g., buying coins or digital items)
    * Recurring Subscriptions (e.g., $9.99/month for premium)
    * E-commerce (Selling physical goods or real-world services)

### 7. The Timeline
* **Question:** When do you ideally want this app live in the App Stores?
* **Description:** Keep in mind that Apple and Google require time to review and approve apps before the public can download them.
* **Input Type:** Radio Button
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Radio Options:**
    * As soon as possible (Urgent)
    * 2 - 4 months
    * 4 - 6 months
    * No rush, just gathering information

### 8. The Budget
* **Question:** What is your allocated budget for designing and developing the initial version of this app?
* **Description:** Knowing your budget helps us decide whether to use a cross-platform framework or build native separate apps.
* **Input Type:** Select (Dropdown)
* **Input Verification:** Required.
* **Selection Options:** * Under $4,000
    * $4,000 - $8,000
    * $8,000 - $15,000
    * $15,000+
